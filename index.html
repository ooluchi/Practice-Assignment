My favorite meal is Jollof Spaphetti.

Spaghett is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta.[1] It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine.

Ingredients Measurements
Spaghetti 1
Pepper Mix 2 cups
Salt 1/2 tbs
Oil 1/4 cup
Tomato paste 1 satchet
Seasoning 3 cubes

Procedures

  • Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  • Once the spaghetti is tender, take it off and drain.
  • Put the pot back on the cooker and add oil.
  • Add the pepper mixture and tomato paste and allow to fry for a bit.
  • Add salt and seasonings.
  • Add the spaghetti.
  • Mix properly and add a little water to let it cook for a few minutes.
  • Once it is ready, serve with your perferred protein
